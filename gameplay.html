Core Mechanics

Run, jump, and climb your way through challenging levels. Pao's signature move is the "Bamboo Bounce," allowing him to reach high platforms!

Power-Ups

Collect glowing bamboo shoots for temporary invincibility and a speed boost. Find hidden chili peppers to unleash a fiery sneeze that can defeat multiple enemies at once!

Enemies

Face off against the mischievous bamboo rat army. Each rat has a unique behavior, from the basic scurrying grunt to the spear-throwing scout.
