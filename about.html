The Story

In a mystical land of towering bamboo forests, our hero, Pao the Panda, must embark on a quest to recover the stolen Golden Bamboo Shoots. An ancient prophecy foretold that these shoots grant immense power, and a shadowy league of mischievous bamboo rats has stolen them to reshape the forest to their will. It's up to Pao to restore balance!

The Team

We are a small, passionate team of indie developers who love retro gaming. Panda Quest is our love letter to the classic platformers we grew up with. We hope you enjoy playing it as much as we enjoyed making it!
